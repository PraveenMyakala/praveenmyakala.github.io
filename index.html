About Me

I’m a software engineer and applied AI researcher with a strong interest in building practical, scalable, and responsible intelligent systems. My work sits at the intersection of distributed systems, machine learning, and real-world deployment, with a particular focus on federated learning, edge AI, and trustworthy AI.

I completed my Master’s in Data Science from the University of Colorado Boulder and continue to collaborate on academic research while working on industry-scale systems. I enjoy turning complex ideas into systems that actually work in practice.

What I Do

Software Engineering

Designing and building scalable backend systems, data pipelines, and cloud-native platforms using Python and modern infrastructure tooling.

AI & ML Research

Researching and publishing in areas such as federated learning, edge AI, explainable AI, and privacy-preserving machine learning.
